Introduction to ACP

At Lifeline.mobi, we are proud to offer Accessibility Connectivity Programs (ACP) designed to bring digital access to everyone. At Lifeline.mobi, we're dedicated to providing Accessibility Connectivity Programs (ACP) that bring digital access within reach for all. Our ACP initiatives, including Free Phones and Free Tablets, empower eligible individuals by granting them essential tools to navigate the digital realm. We believe that digital access is a fundamental right, and we're committed to ensuring that no one is left behind. Whether you're a low-income individual or participate in government assistance programs, we're here to make technology accessible. Lifeline.mobi serves as a bridge to opportunities, connecting people to education, jobs, healthcare, and more. Join us in our mission to create a digitally inclusive world.

Eligibility

To qualify for our ACP programs, you must meet certain eligibility criteria. These criteria are designed to ensure that those who need digital access the most can benefit from our services.

  • Low-income individuals
  • Participation in government assistance programs
  • Additional eligibility requirements may apply
